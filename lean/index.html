External AI efforts based on Lean

  • OpenAI: Solving (some) formal math olympiad problems. (paper)
  • Meta AI: Teaching AI advanced mathematical reasoning. (paper)
  • LeanDojo: open source models, datasets, and code for Lean. (paper, video)
  • xAI: adding support for Lean to Grok.
  • Morph labs: developing ML models for Lean and moogle.ai, an AI for searching the Lean mathematical library using natural language.
  • DeepMind: They have formalized a result related to AI safety in Lean. (paper, code)
